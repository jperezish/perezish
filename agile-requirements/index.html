Management said you need to be more agile.

But if you don’t have all the requirements upfront, how will you know what you’re building?

You're responsible for requirements. Maybe you’re a business analyst. Perhaps that’s one of the roles you play. Your organization is starting an agile journey. Or maybe it’s already underway.

Whatever the case may be, something doesn’t feel right. You’re doing the things that were easy to change. What about the hard stuff? Some of these changes are out of your control, but there’s one thing above all you’re concerned with: Requirements.

You have questions:

  • When should I update the requirements?
  • What do the business requirements look like?
  • How do we keep track of requirements traceability?
  • What do I do when the requirements change?
  • Is my role still the same?

How about this one: Are these the questions I should be asking?
